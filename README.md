# platformacctgenerato
mobile-optimized Platform Account Setup Generator for you! Here's what it includes:
Key Features:

Small font titles (1.2rem for h1, scaling down from there)
1% margins throughout the design
Three main categories: CRM, Email Marketing, and Social Media platforms
15 popular platforms across all categories
Step-by-step setup guides for each platform
Copy-to-clipboard functionality for quick access to signup URLs

Platforms Included:

CRM: Salesforce, HubSpot, Zoho CRM, Pipedrive
Email: Gmail/Google Workspace, Outlook/Microsoft 365, Mailchimp, SendGrid
Social Media: LinkedIn, X (Twitter), Instagram, Facebook, TikTok

How to Use:

Select a tab (CRM, Email, or Social Media)
Click on your desired platform
Fill in your business details
Click "Generate Setup Guide"
Get customized step-by-step instructions with signup links

The app features a modern gradient design, smooth animations, and is fully responsive for mobile devices. All the setup guides are tailored to 2026 standards with current best practices!
